Introduction to Web Development | DEV109

Adam Brehm

Before this class, I really did not know anything about web development. I took a short online course on HTML, but that is the only topic I had understand just a little. However, I knew a fair amount about the structure and build of the internet and how websites worked.

After spending time a few months in this class, web devlopment is far much more interesting than I've imagined. I learned about the big three fundamentals, being HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With each new concept and technique I grasp, my enthusiasm for web development continues to grow, and I look forward to exploring even more aspects of this dynamic and ever-evolving domain.
